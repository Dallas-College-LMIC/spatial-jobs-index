Dallas-Ft. Worth Spatial Jobs Index Project

Access to jobs (or the inverse, access to workforce) is a critical question. This project helps answer it through use of travelshed analysis.

A travelshed is the geographic area that can be reached from a specific point, within a specific period of time. For example, a travelshed can show which job centers a resident from a particular neighborhood could reach within an hour-long commute.
